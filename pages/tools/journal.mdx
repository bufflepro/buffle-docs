# Journal

A personal space to reflect and document your thoughts, experiences, and insights. The Journal feature in Buffle Pro allows you to capture your daily reflections in a structured and accessible format, encouraging mindfulness and self-discovery.

## Features:
1. **Rich Text** – Utilize rich text formatting to enhance your entries with bold, italics, lists, and more, making your writing clear and engaging.
2. **Sync Across Devices** – Access your journal entries from any device, ensuring your thoughts are always within reach, no matter where you are.

## Why Do You Need This Tool?
Keeping a journal can foster self-awareness and emotional clarity. By regularly documenting your thoughts and experiences, you create a valuable resource for personal growth and reflection.
