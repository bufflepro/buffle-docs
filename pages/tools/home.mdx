# Home

**Your central hub for productivity.** The Home feature in Buffle Pro provides a convenient glance at all your tools, allowing you to access each one with just a click. Stay organized and focused as you navigate through your tasks, notes, and more, all from a single, easy-to-use interface.

## Upcoming Features
1. **Reorder Home Widgets** – Customize your dashboard by rearranging home widgets to prioritize the tools you use most.
2. **Add/Delete Home Widgets** – Easily add or remove widgets to tailor your home experience to your needs.
