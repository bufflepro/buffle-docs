# Privacy Policy

**Your privacy is important to us.**

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Chrome extension, Buffle Pro.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

* **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our extension, such as the features you access, the frequency of use, and the websites you visit while the extension is active. This data is collected to improve the user experience and identify potential issues.
* **Device Information:** Basic information about your device, such as the operating system and browser version. This information helps us ensure compatibility and optimize performance.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

* **Improving the Extension:** Analyzing usage data to identify areas for improvement and enhance the functionality of Buffle Pro.
* **Troubleshooting and Support:** Using device information to diagnose and resolve technical issues you may encounter.
* **Security and Fraud Prevention:** Monitoring usage patterns to detect and prevent malicious activity.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as analytics providers. These providers are obligated to keep your information confidential.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

* **Access:** Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
* **Rectification:** Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
* **Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal information under certain circumstances.
* **Restriction:** Request restriction of processing of your personal information.
* **Data Portability:** Request transfer of your personal information to another controller.
* **Object:** Object to the processing of your personal information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website or within the extension itself.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at [support@buffle.pro](mailto:support@buffle.pro).

By using Buffle Pro, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
